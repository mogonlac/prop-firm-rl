The trailing drawdown follows your highest balance. Once triggered, the account is blown.

## Payout Paths

### Standard Path
- Minimum 5 winning trading days with profit ≥ $150 each
- Payout = 50% of account balance, capped at **$5,000**
- 90/10 profit split (you keep 90%)
- Costs: **$49/month** subscription + **$149 activation fee**

### Consistency (XFA) Path
- Minimum 3 days traded
- Largest single day ≤ 40% of total profit
- Payout = 50% of account balance, capped at **$6,000**
- 90/10 profit split
- Costs: **$109/month** subscription, **no activation fee**

---

## Back2Funded

- After an MLL breach, you receive **2 free reactivations**
- Reactivation resets balance to $0, trailing DD resets
- No additional fee for the 2 free reactivations

---

## Key Risk Notes

- At 30 contracts, a single SL of 18 ticks = **$2,700 loss** — exceeds MLL
- Trailing DD locks in as balance grows; a bad day at scale can instantly blow the account
- Consistency path payout cap ($6,000) is higher than Standard ($5,000)
